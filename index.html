About Me

I proudly earned my IT degree from the esteemed University of Ghana. However, life had a different plan for me. Instead of diving straight into a career as a developer, I embarked on a transformative journey.

I dedicated my time and skills to volunteer as an IT and English tutor in village schools across Africa, empowering young minds with the wonders of education.
But that's not all! I also had the privilege of serving as a project manager for an inspiring NGO called Becky's Foundation. Together, we embarked on various initiatives aimed at making a positive difference in communities. From educational programs to community development projects, I took charge, ensuring that these endeavors had a lasting impact.
Now, I'm ready to embrace the next chapter. It's time for me to fully immerse myself in the world of coding. With my dedication and commitment, I aim to craft innovative solutions and contribute to the field of software development. One line of code at a time
